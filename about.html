Hallo~ I am Ruby!

I am a product designer based in New York City, originally from Bay Area, California. I graduated from NYU Stern School of Business with a major in Marketing and Management and a minor in Digital Art & Design.

My career began in IBM Consulting Field Marketing, where I managed various projects, built my team's internal webpage, and became the go-to person for creating visually compelling slides. Along the way, I discovered my passion for UI/UX — crafting designs that balance aesthetics with usability.

I've since built skills in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and design thinking. Adaptive, creative, and eager to grow, I'm excited to contribute designs that leave people saying "That just makes sense."

In my free time, I like to dance, doodle on my iPad, and explore NYC coffee shops!
